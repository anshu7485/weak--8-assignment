Welcome to the RAG Chatbot Documentation

This is a sample document for testing the RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) chatbot.

About RAG:
RAG combines retrieval and generation to provide accurate answers based on your documents.
It first searches for relevant information in your document collection, then uses that information to generate responses.

Features:
- Document loading from multiple formats (PDF, TXT)
- Vector-based similarity search
- Context-aware responses
- Chat history management

How to use:
1. Load your documents using the sidebar
2. Ask questions about the content
3. Get answers based on the document information

This chatbot can help you quickly find information from your document collection.
